One ticket, start to Done. Engineers make 3 moves. Everything between is agents & automation.

This is our delivery flow for a Story: a human writes the intent and holds the two approval gates; AI agents do the drafting and the building in their own cloud sandboxes; deterministic automation carries every signal between them. Three actors — watch who does what.
